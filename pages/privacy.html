Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2025

At Grind to Green, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at grindtogreen.com.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name and email address if you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us directly.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our site, such as pages viewed, time spent, and links clicked, collected via cookies or analytics tools.
  • Device & Browser Info: IP address, browser type, operating system, and other technical details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Send updates, blog content, and promotions via email (only if you opt in)
  • Monitor website traffic and understand audience behavior through analytics
  • Ensure site security and prevent misuse

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to personalize your experience and analyze website traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but some parts of the site may not function properly.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools like:

  • Google Analytics: To track site usage and visitor behavior
  • Email Services: To manage subscriptions and send newsletters
  • Advertising Partners: Such as Google AdSense, to serve relevant ads

These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not control how they manage data.

5. Data Protection

We take data security seriously and use industry-standard methods to protect your personal information. However, no method of online transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of email marketing at any time
  • Disable cookies in your browser
  • Request access, update, or deletion of your personal data

7. Children’s Privacy

Grind to Green is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in technology, law, or our services. We’ll post the updated version with a new effective date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our contact form or email us at hello@grindtogreen.com.
