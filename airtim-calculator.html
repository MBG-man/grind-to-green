How to Convert Airtime to Minutes

Many Nigerians want to know how many minutes their airtime will last. This depends on your network's call rate.

Call Time = Airtime ÷ Rate per second

Example

₦100 airtime at 50 kobo/sec gives about 3 minutes 20 seconds.

Common Call Rates in Nigeria

  • MTN: ~75 kobo/sec
  • Airtel: ~50 kobo/sec
  • Glo: ~60 kobo/sec
